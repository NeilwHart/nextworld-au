About us

We are a small group of people doing bible study, research and supporting each other, grow in faith, and carry out God's work together. Verse below shows the significance of fellowship, encouragement, accountability, and love within small groups of believers.

Acts 2:42

“They devoted themselves to the apostles' teaching and to fellowship, to the breaking of bread and to prayer.”

Verse above also describes how people followed Jesus's desciples. Small groups have a very important role in being a guide to others, in a confused and sensless world. We ought to teach or guide learners simple things in Christianity. Like the breaking of bread at His last supper. Washing of the feet. Greeting one another in Jesus's name, etc.

Hebrews 10:24-25

“And let us consider how we may spur one another on toward love and good deeds, not giving up meeting together, as some are in the habit of doing, but encouraging one another-and all the more as you see the Day approaching.”

This verse strongly emphasis the need to encourage and meet one another on a daily basis. But some members may see it as monotonus or unnecessary or may be having other committments and may not be able to meet regularly. Yes, now a days with job and family life, it may not be possible. But we can try and God can see how hard we are trying.

1 Peter 5:8

Be sober, be vigilant; because your adversary the devil walks about like a roaring lion, seeking whom he may devour.

This is the reason why out group meets often in the name of Jesus because the children of devil are waiting to pounce on you, to take you into distraction and deveate you mind from Christ. There are only a few Christians left and it's going to be difficult to stay away from sin.

1 Corinthians 10:13

No temptation has overtaken you except such as is common to man; but God is faithful, who will not allow you to be tempted beyond what you are able, but with the temptation will also make the way of escape, that you may be able to bear it.

It is hard to find the truth in this world, now a days and it's only going to get worse. That's why we need God's hand even more, in times like this. So have God first in everything you do and He will make a way to excape as He has promised in the verse above. Keep in mind the verse below and you will have something to look forward, in times of darkness.

Verse below is one of the last letter's written by Peter giving us an insight to the future.

2 Peter 3:11-13

Since everything will be destroyed in this way, what kind of people ought you to be? You ought to conduct yourselves in holiness and godliness as you anticipate and hasten the coming of the day of God, when the heavens will be destroyed by fire and the elements will melt in the heat. But in keeping with God's promise, we are looking forward to a new heaven and a new earth, where righteousness dwells.
